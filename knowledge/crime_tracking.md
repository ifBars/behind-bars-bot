# Crime Tracking Guide

## Overview

The Behind Bars mod tracks all crimes you commit and maintains a comprehensive criminal record system.

## Crime Detection

The mod automatically detects various crimes:
- **Assault on Civilians**: Attacking non-hostile NPCs
- **Drug Possession**: Carrying illegal drugs
- **Manslaughter**: Accidental killing
- **Murder**: Intentional killing
- **Witness Intimidation**: Threatening witnesses

## Wanted Level

- **Visual Indicator**: Shows your current wanted status
- **0-5 Stars**: Higher stars mean more serious crimes
- **Affects**: How aggressively police pursue you

## Rap Sheet System

Your rap sheet is a complete criminal record that tracks:

### Crime Records
- **All Crimes**: Every crime you've committed
- **Details Include**:
  - Crime type
  - Timestamp
  - Location
  - Fine amount
  - Jail time served
  - Severity level

### Parole Records
- **Current Parole**: Active parole status
- **Past Parole**: History of previous parole periods
- **Violations**: All parole violations

### Violation Records
- **Contraband Possession**: When you were caught with illegal items
- **Other Infractions**: Various rule violations
- **Timestamps**: When violations occurred

## Persistent Storage

- **Saves Across Sessions**: Your criminal record persists
- **File Location**: Stored in game data files
- **Multiplayer**: Each player has their own rap sheet

## How Your Record Affects You

### Jail Sentences
- More crimes = longer sentences
- Serious crimes = higher severity
- Repeat offenses = stiffer penalties

### Parole Assessment
- Criminal history affects LSI level
- More crimes = higher risk assessment
- Violations increase supervision level

### Bail Amounts
- Criminal history may affect bail calculations
- Repeat offenders may face higher bail

## Contraband Detection

The system automatically detects illegal items:
- **During Searches**: Parole officers can find contraband
- **During Booking**: Items are detected when you're arrested
- **Types Detected**:
  - Drugs
  - Weapons
  - Stolen goods

## Witness System

- **Witness Tracking**: NPCs can witness your crimes
- **Witness Reports**: Witnesses report crimes to authorities
- **Affects**: May increase your wanted level

## Viewing Your Record

- **Rap Sheet**: Access your complete criminal history
- **Current Status**: See your wanted level
- **Parole Info**: Check your parole status and violations

## Tips

- Your criminal record never goes away
- Multiple crimes increase your risk level
- Avoid witnesses when committing crimes
- Contraband is always detected during searches
- Clean records lead to lower LSI levels
