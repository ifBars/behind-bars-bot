# Jail System Guide

## Overview

The Behind Bars mod adds a comprehensive jail system to Schedule I. When you're arrested, you'll go through a complete booking process and serve time in a realistic jail facility.

## Getting Arrested

1. **Commit a Crime**: The mod automatically detects when you commit crimes in-game
2. **Severity Assessment**: Your crime is automatically assessed for severity:
   - **Minor**: Small offenses, short sentences
   - **Moderate**: Medium-level crimes
   - **Major**: Serious offenses, longer sentences
   - **Severe**: Most serious crimes, longest sentences
3. **Choose Your Punishment**: You can choose between:
   - **Pay a Fine**: If you have enough money
   - **Serve Jail Time**: If you can't afford the fine

## Jail Facilities

The jail includes:
- **Cells**: Individual cells for prisoners
- **Common Room**: Shared area for inmates
- **Booking Area**: Where you're processed after arrest
- **Guard Room**: Where guards monitor the facility
- **Storage Area**: Where your personal belongings are stored

## Booking Process

When you're arrested, you'll go through a complete booking process:

1. **Mugshot Station**: Your photo is taken
2. **Scanner Station**: Your fingerprints are scanned
3. **Inventory Processing**: Your personal items are stored securely
4. **Cell Assignment**: You're assigned to a cell based on your sentence length

### Short Sentences (< 1 day)

- Quick processing
- No inventory storage needed

## Jail Time

- **Real-time Countdown**: Watch your sentence count down in real-time
- **Game Time Conversion**: 
  - 1 real second = 1 game minute
  - 1 real minute = 1 game hour
  - 24 real minutes = 1 game day
- **Jail Info UI**: Shows your remaining time, crime details, and bail amount

## Early Release

You may be eligible for early release based on:
- Good behavior
- Work programs (if available)
- Other factors determined by the system

## Security Systems

The jail includes:
- **Security Cameras**: Monitor all areas
- **Automated Doors**: Controlled by palm scanners
- **Guard Patrols**: Guards monitor the facility
- **Lighting Control**: Dynamic lighting for day/night cycles

## Tips

- Higher-level players face stiffer penalties
- Barons can't escape with petty fines
- Jail time scales with your player level
- Your criminal record affects future sentences
